# AI SEO Playbook for B2B SaaS

## Executive Summary

This playbook combines research from Matt Diggity, Nathan Gotch, Ryan Law, and Kevin Indig to create a practical AI SEO framework for B2B SaaS companies.

The objective is to improve AI Search visibility, increase organic growth, and build scalable content operations using AI-assisted workflows while maintaining human editorial quality.

---

# Step 1: Build a Strong Technical Foundation

Before creating content, ensure:

* Important pages are crawlable
* Internal linking is strong
* Page speed is optimized
* Schema markup is implemented
* AI crawlers can access content

### Tools

* Google Search Console
* Ahrefs
* ChatGPT
* Schema Validator

---

# Step 2: Select the Right Topics

Focus on:

* Commercial intent keywords
* Comparison pages
* Alternative pages
* Product-focused content
* Industry-specific problems

Examples:

* Best CRM Software
* HubSpot Alternatives
* Salesforce vs HubSpot

### Tools

* Ahrefs
* ChatGPT
* Google Search

---

# Step 3: Create Content Briefs

Every article should have:

* Target keyword
* Search intent
* Key questions
* Competitor analysis
* Product mentions
* Examples and use cases

A strong brief creates better AI-generated content.

---

# Step 4: Use AI for Draft Creation

Use AI to:

* Generate outlines
* Create first drafts
* Expand sections
* Generate FAQs
* Improve readability

Recommended workflow:

Brief → Outline → Draft → Review

### Tools

* ChatGPT
* Claude

---

# Step 5: Human Editorial Review

Never publish AI-generated content without review.

Review for:

* Accuracy
* Examples
* Brand voice
* Clarity
* SEO optimization

Human review remains mandatory.

---

# Step 6: Optimize Content Structure

AI systems prefer:

* Direct answers
* Question-based headings
* FAQ sections
* Lists and bullet points
* Clear definitions

Avoid long introductions.

Place key information near the beginning of the article.

---

# Step 7: Add Internal Links and Schema

Implement:

* Article Schema
* FAQ Schema
* HowTo Schema

Add internal links to:

* Product pages
* Comparison pages
* High-value resources

### Tools

* Ahrefs Site Audit
* Schema Validator

---

# Step 8: Distribution Strategy

Do not rely only on Google.

Distribute through:

* LinkedIn
* YouTube
* Newsletters
* Industry Communities
* Review Platforms

The more trusted mentions a brand receives, the more likely it is to appear in AI-generated answers.

---

# Step 9: Measure AI Visibility

Track:

* AI referral traffic
* AI citations
* AI Overview appearances
* Organic traffic
* Conversion rate

Create separate reporting for AI visibility.

### Tools

* Ahrefs
* Google Search Console
* Analytics Platforms

# Common Risks

1. AI hallucinations
2. Incorrect links
3. Weak fact checking
4. Poor content quality
5. Publishing without review

Always verify AI-generated output before publication.

* Technical SEO audit
* Improve internal linking
* Add schema markup
* Create content strategy

## Month 2

* Publish comparison content
* Publish alternative pages
* Launch AI-assisted workflow

## Month 3

* Measure AI visibility
* Improve content performance
* Expand distribution channels

---

# Final Recommendation

AI SEO is not a replacement for traditional SEO.

The most successful B2B SaaS teams combine:

* Strong technical foundations
* AI-assisted content production
* Human editorial oversight
* Consistent distribution
* AI visibility tracking

Organizations that treat AI as part of a structured operating system will outperform those using AI only as a writing tool.
